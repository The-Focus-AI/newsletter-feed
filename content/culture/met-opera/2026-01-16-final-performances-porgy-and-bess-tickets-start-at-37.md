THE GERSHWINS’

**Porgy and Bess**

FINAL PERFORMANCES: JANUARY 16, 22, 24*

With its fusion of opera, jazz, and Broadway, the Gershwins’ timeless masterpiece about the joys and struggles of a Black community in Charleston features tunes whose popularity has transcended the opera house, including the classics “Summertime,” “My Man’s Gone Now,” and “It Ain’t Necessarily So.”

Bass-baritone Alfred Walker and soprano Brittany Renee join forces as the star-crossed Porgy and Bess, headlining an electrifying production brought to vivid life with choreography by Tony Award–nominee Camille A. Brown.

https://www.metopera.org/season/2025-26-season/porgy-and-bess/ 

*Please note the performance on January 24 is currently sold out although returns may become available. A limited number of standing room tickets will also go on sale day of at 10AM.

BELLINI

**I Puritani**

ENCORE SCREENINGS WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 14*

**"A new golden age of bel canto singing" —

**Financial Times****

Don't miss your chance to experience the Met's new production of Bellini's final masterpiece, back in select U.S. movie theaters this Wednesday, January 14.*

Soprano Lisette Oropesa and tenor Lawrence Brownlee star as the young lovers caught in the crossfire of the English Civil War, in an "exceptionally sung new production" (

**The New York Times**) directed by Charles Edwards. Marco Armiliato conducts an exciting cast that also features baritone Ricardo José Rivera and bass-baritone Christian Van Horn.

https://www.metopera.org/season/in-cinemas/2025-26-season/i-puritani/ 

https://www.metopera.org/Season/In-Cinemas/Theater-Finder/ 

If you live in an area without access to a participating movie theater, you may be eligible to access this performance via The Met: Live at Home https://www.metopera.org/season/in-cinemas/the-met-live-at-home/, the Met's streaming platform. 

**Live at Home** performances are available to watch live and on demand for seven days following the performance.

An English transcript of the live transmission's spoken host introduction and interview segments will be available to view here https://www.metopera.org/season/in-cinemas/live-in-hd-transcripts/. Encore screenings will also include open captions.

*Encore dates may vary at select locations nationwide.

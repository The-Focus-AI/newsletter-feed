A massive thanks to everyone who contributed directly or via Venmo to [Food for All](https://foodforallservices.org/) in Maine this past week — we raised over $16k, which will help provide Halal & culturally specific food for 300 families sheltering-in-place because of ICE surveillance. I've linked to the donation receipt [in the General CS Chat](https://www.patreon.com/messages/158858d170bc4ed6a604b7f47d83ab2b?mode=campaign&tab=chats&utm_id=0d77659f-f4e9-4213-b3c6-b6aabcc502c9&utm_medium=email). So long as ICE keeps making life miserable for our neighbors, we'll keep trying to make it less miserable — next week we'll turn our focus to rent funds. I have some leads but if you're involved with an organization struggling to meet demand, send me an email [(annehelenpetersen@gmail.com](mailto:(annehelenpetersen@gmail.com)).

Half the country got snow and ice and we got: clear blue skies and the perfect winter temperature of 34 degrees. We got light blankets of frost and morning fires in the fireplace and alpenglow on Mt. Baker. If your power went out or you're battling stubborn ice, I'm sorry and I hope the melt comes soon. But as weeks of rain and grey return to the PNW, I'm grateful for the respite — and for a quick lark of a trip down to Portland with friends to go to an in-person dahlia sale that had the energy of a Tickle-Me-Elmo-era Black Friday. (We didn't really go for the tubers, we went for the change of scenery and [some really good Russian food](https://www.npr.org/sections/thesalt/2017/11/18/564397498/kachka-the-word-that-saved-a-family-during-wwii-and-inspired-a-chef)).

I've also amassed a healthy collection of links/recs — which is behind the paywall because for whatever reason stuff like this prompts people to actually subscribe to the stuff they read and love every week.

If that's you: welcome. If you're already a subscriber: thank you, you make this work sustainable. Read on for two bonkers book recs, a good replacement for The Americans, a delicious spicy dry ramen, how to force Gmail AI to stop summarizing your emails....and should we all make the same lasagna in February and talk about it? Plus a bunch of other good reads & just this week's Just Trust Me...

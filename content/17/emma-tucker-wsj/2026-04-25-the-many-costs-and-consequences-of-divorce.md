For Jessica Simon, the first signs that her marriage was in trouble showed up in the checking account she shared with her husband. It would sometimes be just a stray DraftKings charge. Over time, more charges began to add up to a tipping point.

“[The Cost of Divorce](https://trk.wsj.com/click/45382424.142312/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cud3NqLmNvbS9wZXJzb25hbC1maW5hbmNlL3RoZS1jb3N0LW9mLWRpdm9yY2UtZWQ4YWMzZWY_bW9kPWRqZW1XZWVrZW5k/69200153f085751e04b997c2Be61f30ea)” is the name of a new series examining what tears marriages apart, and the many directions the damaging ripples spread.

 

This week we also look at a couple who built their dream home within the facade of an historic church, and new technology helping search for extraterrestrial life.

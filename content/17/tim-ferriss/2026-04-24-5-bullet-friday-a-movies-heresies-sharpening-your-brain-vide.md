Eat Drink Man Woman (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/p8heh9h4oz0wqdsq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1fTVFPRHRVVzhrMCZyY289MQ==

*very* hungry. For the film nerds, it’s one of the early Ang Lee
(
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/x0hph6he0w6vqdi5/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvQW5nX0xlZQ==

Mountain; Life of Pi, etc.) that put him on the global map. For
the language nerds, you’ll hear many different flavors of
Mandarin, plus one showcase of Taiwanese.

From the description: “Master Chef Chu (Sihung Lung (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/6qheh8hle7g89dio/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvU2lodW5nX0x1bmc=
)) is semi-retired and lives at home with his three unmarried
daughters, Jia-Jen (Kuei-Mei Yang (

)), a religious chemistry teacher; Jia-Chien (Chien-Lien Wu (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/58hvh7hg2m8d65c6/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvSmFja2x5bl9XdQ==
)), an airline executive; and Jia-Ning (Yu-Wen Wang (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/25h2hoh3w2zpm2i3/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvV2FuZ19ZdS13ZW5fKFRhaXdhbmVzZV9hY3RyZXNzKQ==

What I’m reading
----------------

“Some Contemporary Heresies (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/qvh8h7hdpr5mkdil/aHR0cHM6Ly9rZXZpbmtlbGx5LnN1YnN0YWNrLmNvbS9wL3NvbWUtY29udGVtcG9yYXJ5LWhlcmVzaWVz
)” by Kevin Kelly (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/g3hnh5hmw358q6sr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1lWjNZX3JqS3ZJRQ==
). Having known Kevin a long time, I suspect that these are
collected from some very smart people. Smart doesn’t always equal
right, but it does add a little color to the wild statements.
“Faith in the Possible (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/9qhzhnhdrpmv59t9/aHR0cHM6Ly9yZWlkaG9mZm1hbi5zdWJzdGFjay5jb20vcC9mYWl0aC1pbi10aGUtcG9zc2libGU_bWNfY2lkPTAzNGEwNzI3NGU=
).” This is the first article on Reid Hoffman (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/3ohphkh3g7l5pvsr/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDE1LzA4LzMxL3RoZS1vcmFjbGUtb2Ytc2lsaWNvbi12YWxsZXktcmVpZC1ob2ZmbWFuLXBsdXMtbWljaGFlbC1tY2N1bGxvdWdoLw==
)’s new Substack Theory of the Game. This piece has a good
fine-slicing of the religion of techno-optimism, which is
reframed as creation. His description of the various religious
factions is very good, and I hadn’t seen it put so succinctly
before. One important footnote: read the comments. I’m sharing
this article for the thought-provoking comments as much as I am
for the piece itself. Some of them provide alternative lenses and
more critical views of startups as engines of progress.

Science I’m following
---------------------

“Ocular Glymphatic System: Its Modulation and Role in Intraocular
Tumors (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4uq73eo9uvhx0z4o0u6h64282rzclhdnq/l2hehmhl739mxxb6/aHR0cHM6Ly9pb3ZzLmFydm9qb3VybmFscy5vcmcvYXJ0aWNsZS5hc3B4P2FydGljbGVpZD0yODExNjk0
)” by Dr. Jasmine H. Francis and Dr. David H. Abramson. The
glymphatic system is worth getting familiar with. Excerpt: “The
glymphatic system is a glial-based perivascular network that
clears metabolic waste from the central nervous system (CNS), and
its dysfunction is related to neurodegenerative disease.” New
therapies for enhancing CNS glymphatic flow are discussed in this
piece.

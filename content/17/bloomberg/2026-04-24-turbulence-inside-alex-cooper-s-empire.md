A simmering feud with mega-influencer Alix Earle isn’t the only source of drama
 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-20/alix-earle-feud-isn-t-alex-cooper-s-only-problem-as-unwell-network-struggles>
 surrounding Alex Cooper’s podcast empire.

Lately, Cooper — the host of Call Her Daddy, the fourth-most popular podcast 
in the US — has been grappling with multiple challenges inside her business, 
including employee turnover, a sputtering slate of shows and discord between 
staff members and her husband and co-CEO Matt Kaplan, who’s earned a reputation 
for yelling at employees, according to people familiar with the company’s 
operations.

This week, the company’s chief marketing officer held an all-hands meeting 
amid growing concerns — butCooper and Kaplan did not attend 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-22/alex-cooper-husband-skip-team-meeting-after-behavior-complaints>

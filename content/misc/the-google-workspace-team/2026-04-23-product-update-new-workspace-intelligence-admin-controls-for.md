# [Product Update] New Workspace Intelligence admin controls for Google Workspace starting Apr 22, 2026

**From:** The Google Workspace Team <workspace-noreply@google.com>
**Date:** Wed, 22 Apr 2026 19:02:43 -0700

---

Google Workspace logo

Review new Workspace Intelligence features and admin settings.

Dear administrator,

You're receiving this communication because we're introducing Workspace  
Intelligence. Workspace Intelligence is an underlying AI system that  
provides Gemini with a real-time understanding of your work across Google  
Workspace.

With Workspace Intelligence, all of your generative AI tasks within  
Workspace will be grounded in your Workspace data across Gmail, Chat,  
Calendar, and Drive, eliminating the need for users to manually provide  
context to Gemini on every query.

For example, imagine you want to draft an update to your manager on  
“Project X” in Google Docs. You simply ask Gemini in Docs. Workspace  
Intelligence will consult all relevant data, your meeting notes,  
documentation, and chat discussions about Project X, and draft the report  
in seconds, with full citations.

As an administrator, you have control over which data sources Workspace  
Intelligence can use for your organization. We understand this change may  
require some adjustments and have provided additional information below to  
guide you through these new controls.

What this means for your organization

Workspace Intelligence securely draws from content in Gmail, Drive, Chat,  
and Calendar, as well as connected third party sources, to deliver  
personalized outputs via Gemini 3 reasoning.

Starting April 22, 2026:

New Admin Controls: You will have access to new controls in the Admin  
console to manage which data sources are used to ground Workspace  
Intelligence. Note: it may take up to 3 days after rollout begins for these  
controls to appear.
Default Setting: The Workspace Intelligence settings will be default ON for  
all users in your organization.

What's not changing:

These changes do not alter our underlying data handling practices or  
privacy commitments. AI features respect user-level content access,  
ensuring that responses are grounded only in content that a user already  
has permission to view. Your data is never used to train generative AI  
models or for advertising purposes.

Additional Details:

Workspace Intelligence powers AI capabilities, such as AI Overviews in  
Gmail and Drive, and Help me create in Docs, by securely accessing content  
across Gmail, Drive, and Calendar.
New Admin console settings will allow admins to decide at the  
organizational unit and group level which data sources (Gmail, Chat,  
Calendar, or Drive) may be actively searched by Gemini features in  
Workspace.
If enabled, the AI features respect user-level content access, ensuring  
that responses are grounded only in content owned by or shared with that  
user.
If you turn off Workspace Intelligence for a specific data source,  
generative AI features will not actively search for content from the  
disabled source. However, if a user adds a specific source to their prompt,  
that source will be used in Gemini's response.

For example, if you turn Drive off as a source, users can still ask Gemini  
about specific files. Gemini will consult these files, but it won't  
actively search for other files. Additionally, if you turn off certain data  
sources, users may lose access to specific AI features.

What you need to do

No action is required unless you'd like to change the default settings. To  
modify the Workspace Intelligence Admin settings, follow the steps in this  
Help Center article.

We're here to help

If you have questions, please review the Help Center article linked to  
above.

Thanks for choosing Google Workspace.

– The Google Workspace Team

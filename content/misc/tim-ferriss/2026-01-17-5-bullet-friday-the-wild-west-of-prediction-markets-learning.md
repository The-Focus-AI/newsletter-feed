)) are also excellent.

From Wikipedia:

Michel Thomas (born Moniek Kroskof, February 3, 1914–January 8,
2005) was a polyglot linguist and decorated war veteran. He
survived imprisonment in several Nazi concentration camps after
serving in the Maquis of the French Resistance and worked with
the U.S. Army Counter Intelligence Corps during World War II.
After the war, Thomas immigrated to the United States, where he
developed a language-teaching system known as the “Michel Thomas
method.” In 2004, he was awarded the Silver Star by the United
States Army.

Related content I've been bingeing
----------------------------------

YouTube: “The Language Master, a film about Michel Thomas (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/v8uq2mz3durhvmgmvgh5av3xwmllh9h6x0/l2hehmhlq4w7r4s6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_ZmVhdHVyZT15b3V0dS5iZSZ2PW1yX05VV2hZQVNn
).” This is a great place to start to get a taste of what his
method looks like in a live classroom. It also demonstrates how
stubborn and difficult he could be! For propagating his own
method, he was sometimes his own worst enemy.

Book: The Test Of Courage: Michel Thomas (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/v8uq2mz3durhvmgmvgh5av3xwmllh9h6x0/m2h7h5h329wze0am/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9UZXN0LUNvdXJhZ2UtQmlvZ3JhcGh5LU5hemktSHVudGVyLUNocmlzdG9waGVyLWVib29rL2RwL0IwMDcxTUIwMjIvP3RhZz1vZmZzaXRvZnRpbWZlLTIw
), a biography of the Holocaust survivor and Nazi hunter by
Christopher Robbins. I found this book to be gratifying, and
Robbins did a beautiful job with research and fact-checking
around details of WWII and the Holocaust I had never seen before.
How do we not learn this stuff in school? The citations and
footnotes reflect a tremendous amount of work by the author, and
he makes a lot of Michel’s life a compelling page-turner that
reads like a thriller.

Alas, it also seems clear that Michel himself was prone to
exaggeration and possibly occasional fabrication. Some of his
theatrical twists and anecdotes simply don’t add up, which is a
shame, as his 100% verifiable experiences alone are ridiculously
sufficient for an incredible story! Sadly, some people can’t help
themselves. It didn’t prevent me from highlighting a lot of the
research notes on Kindle, and his language method’s results
literally speak for themselves. Check out the above documentary
first.

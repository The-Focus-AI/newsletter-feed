January Special: No Joining Fee!

This January, the Y is waiving the $60 joining fee for new members. It’s a
great time to invite friends, family, or coworkers to experience everything
the Y has to offer—from fitness and wellness to swimming, classes, and
community connection. Help spread the word and start the year stronger
together.

We’re closely monitoring the incoming storm and its potential impact on our
operations. If there are any delays or closures, we’ll share updates as
soon as possible via email and on our Facebook page. Thank you for your
patience and understanding as we prioritize safety for our members and
staff.

Easy Tai Chi in Canaan

Tuesdays at Noon+

Find your balance and calm your mind with our Easy Tai Chi class at the
Canaan Y. This gentle, low-impact class focuses on slow, flowing movements
that improve flexibility, coordination, and inner peace. Perfect for
beginners and all fitness levels — join us and move toward better health
and harmony!

Lifeguard Training Course

Recertification Class: February 22

Full Certification Course: March 14 - 15

Become a certified lifeguard and gain the skills needed to help keep our
community safe. This American Red Cross–aligned course includes CPR, First
Aid, and hands-on rescue training. Participants complete online coursework
before in-person sessions at the Torrington YMCA. Space is limited—register
early.

The Peggy O’Brien Empowerment Project for Emerging Leaders

Presented by YMCA Tall Timbers & Camp M.O.E.

The YMCA Tall Timbers Emerging Leaders program empowers teens ages 13+ to
grow as confident leaders all year long. This program helps youth gain
real-world job skills, mental health resilience, and hands-on leadership
opportunities in the community. From workshops on teamwork, communication,
and job readiness to youth-led service projects, participants develop the
tools they need to succeed in school, work, and life. [5]Contact Julie for
more information about this new project!

The Plunge at Camp M.O.E

Saturday, February 28

The Plunge at Camp M.O.E. is coming up on February 28, and it’s all about
supporting kids who need camp the most. Make a splash by donating, forming
a team, or joining a team to raise funds that help send local youth to
summer camp. Every dollar makes a difference—even if you stay dry! Donate

Zumba in Torrington

Mondays at 6 PM | Wednesdays at 7 PM

Dancing is best described as a fun, joyful Latin inspired fitness class
that combines all the rhythms in Latin music. Zumba applies dance moves
from Salsa, Cumbia, Merengue, Rumba, Mambo, Belly Dance, Flamenco, Samba,
Tango & Reggaetone.

YMCA Tall Timbers Outdoor Center

Start the new year by planning your next celebration at YMCA Tall Timbers
Outdoor Center! Nestled on 174 acres of scenic woodland and waterfront in
Torrington, Tall Timbers offers versatile rental options — from spacious
pavilions and peaceful trails to an indoor lodge with a full kitchen that
accommodates up to 120 guests.

Whether you’re dreaming of a wedding, family reunion, corporate retreat,
or special gathering, our beautiful natural setting and flexible event
spaces make it easy to create unforgettable memories in 2026. Contact us
today to reserve your date!

February Personal Training Special!

Train together this February at the Y. Members who purchase personal
training sessions can bring a friend or partner to train alongside them at
no additional cost. Enjoy personalized coaching, shared motivation, and the
energy of working toward your goals together—all in one session with one of
our certified personal trainers!

Splash Week at the Canaan Family YMCA

February 23 - 27

Splash Week is a fun and affordable way to introduce kids to swim lessons
while building important water safety skills. This weeklong program helps
children gain confidence in the water while learning basic swim skills in a
supportive environment. Early swim exposure plays a key role in keeping
kids safer around pools, lakes, and waterfronts.

Lifeguard Training Course

Recertification Class: February 22

Full Certification Course: March 14 - 15

Become a certified lifeguard and gain the skills needed to help keep our
community safe. This American Red Cross–aligned course includes CPR, First
Aid, and hands-on rescue training. Participants complete online coursework
before in-person sessions at the Torrington YMCA. Space is limited—register
early.

The Peggy O’Brien Empowerment Project for Emerging Leaders

Presented by YMCA Tall Timbers & Camp M.O.E.

The YMCA Tall Timbers Emerging Leaders program empowers teens ages 13+ to
grow as confident leaders all year long. This program helps youth gain
real-world job skills, mental health resilience, and hands-on leadership
opportunities in the community. From workshops on teamwork, communication,
and job readiness to youth-led service projects, participants develop the
tools they need to succeed in school, work, and life. [4]Contact Julie for
more information about this new project!

Ninja & Swim Afterschool Program in Torrington

Grades K - 3 Wednesdays | Grades 4 - 6 Fridays

The Torrington YMCA is excited to offer our popular Ninja & Swim
After-School Program for students in grades K-6 from Forbes, Torringford,
Southwest, and Vogel-Wetmore schools! This fun, energy-burning program
gives kids time in the gym and our Ninja Center each week — a great way to
build friendships, stay healthy, and stay active after school.

Next Session begins February 11, register today!

Power Pump in Winsted

Mon at 10:15AM | Wed & Fri at 8:45 AM

Power Pump is a weight-based group-fitness program where each person uses
their own barbell and weight plates. Class is 45 minutes long and is the
ideal workout for anyone looking to get lean, toned and fit – fast. Using
light to moderate weights with lots of repetition, POWER PUMP gives you a
total body workout.

Winter Break Family Swim

February 16 - 20 | 1 PM - 3 PM

The Canaan Family YMCA is offering Drop-In Family Swim during February
vacation, February 16–20, from 1:00–3:00 PM! For just $5 per day/per

YMCA Tall Timbers Outdoor Center

Start the new year by planning your next celebration at YMCA Tall Timbers
Outdoor Center! Nestled on 174 acres of scenic woodland and waterfront in
Torrington, Tall Timbers offers versatile rental options — from spacious
pavilions and peaceful trails to an indoor lodge with a full kitchen that
accommodates up to 120 guests.

Whether you’re dreaming of a wedding, family reunion, corporate retreat,
or special gathering, our beautiful natural setting and flexible event
spaces make it easy to create unforgettable memories in 2026. Contact us
today to reserve your date!

‚Üì‚Üì44Carbon market grows

¬†
Total value of carbon credits bought globally in 2025, up 6% from the previous year. The total volume of credits trading hands has dipped, according to a [new report from carbon market intelligence firm Sylvera](https://semafor.com/s/Za0HfaHutf?email=wschenk@gmail.com), as investors learn to steer clear of projects with dubious green credentials. But the value of high-quality projects is rising, as is their share of total credit trades, with highly rated credits accounting for 31% of trades compared to 25% the previous year. The most important driver of growth in this market are government-mandated purchases, rather than voluntary offsets, the report said; the compulsory side of the market is on track to supersede the voluntary side by 2027.

New: W&B Automations now available to all

W&B Automations <https://email.wandb.ai/MjYxLVFIUC04MjIAAAGhUKFpxEpfznuZaTLhOG4Qtjsc0lSM1DOmFIGENt-002wjUgmtbmQbVlwaaAOpRzzqRgJ2aYg=> let you build event-driven workflows to streamline tasks like model testing and deployment. Check it out!
New: W&B Inference offers Gemma 4

Google’s Gemma 4 is now available through the W&B Weave playground or through the W&B Inference API. The 31B-parameter model performs competitively with models up to 20x larger.
New: W&B Weave now includes agent lifecycle alerts

W&B Weave now lets you set up alerts <https://email.wandb.ai/MjYxLVFIUC04MjIAAAGhUKFpxJF7YHOAgYvkf9yzc0_a41qI7sghGqVlojf9v-Kiw13v16l5Db9fbEbyVaMPAnvPpbI=> that trigger when a metric or score crosses a threshold. You can configure alerts to send a Slack message or fire a webhook, so you can take action immediately. Alerts help you catch regressions such as declining accuracy or increasing toxicity.

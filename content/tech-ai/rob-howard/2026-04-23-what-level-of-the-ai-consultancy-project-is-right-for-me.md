3 – Hybrid Consulting
---------------------

Hybrid Consulting is our most personalized option. It allows us
to work even more closely in 4 specific ways:

* Done-For-You Implementation: Get up to 10 hours of dedicated
hours from our team of senior developers to actually code, build,

problem, solution, or opportunity.

calls (limited to five people) for advanced and/or privacy
sensitive topics. These can be used to address everything from
HIPAA compliance, ‘C-Suite only’ business issues, and NDA-level
material.
* 6 Monthly 1-on-1 Private Coaching Sessions: Book a 30-minute
personal coaching session with our team, up to once a month for
six months.
* Unlimited 1-on-1 Private Messaging for 6 Months: Talk directly
to Rob or the expert AI Consultancy team about anything
in-between calls.

And, since this also includes everything inside of the Training
and Coaching levels, that means access to an unparalleled vault

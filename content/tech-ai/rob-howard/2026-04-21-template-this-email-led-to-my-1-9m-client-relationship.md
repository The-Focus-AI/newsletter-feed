2 Reasons Why Potential Clients Ignore Emails
(and Why You Opened This One)
---------------------------------------------

Trust is invisible…

And yet you, me, and our clients can feel it evaporate from our
body the second we see those emails in our inbox. You know the
kind I'm talking about.

The ones where something just feels…off.

Sometimes it's weird buzzwords and sometimes it's the obvious
copy-paste that's as personal as a parking ticket.

To understand the "why", you could run a quick 'Amazon search' on

bookshelf.

Time is valuable so I'll share 2 client outreach principles I've
distilled from sending over 1 million emails:


-------------
1 – Relevance
-------------

Relevance is the #1 reason you opened this email. Within a
professional context, it is deliberately useful for achieving a
specific goal that matters to you right now (starting and growing
an AI consultancy).

It's the same for…

* Every email that you will open today
* The email that started my $1.9M client relationship
* Emails you'll send to start relationships with your AI
consulting clients

…and it's also true for the emails that none of us will open.

Irrelevant emails get ignored. Period.

----------------
2 – Relationship
----------------

(Quality of) Relationship is the #2 reason we open emails.

We don't need a masterclass on this – just notice your own
behavior. Whether it's your smartphone or laptop, there's a
little something we do 99.99999% of the time before opening an
email.

We do it either (a) just before reading the subject line or (b)
just after reading the subject line.

We ask ourselves…"who sent this to me?"

Then, we look at the sender.

Then, depending on the strength of the relationship, we'll then
ignore other emails to read that one first (if it's an important
relationship).

––

Relevance x Relationship = Why We Open, Read, & Respond To Emails

Every. Single. Time.

It's why you opened this email.

And it's also why the 1-2 punch email strategy works for starting
high-value client relationships.


----------------------------------------------------
The 1-2 Punch Email Strategy: Build Trust From Day 1
----------------------------------------------------

Every great client relationship begins with a spark of trust. And
trust doesn’t come from boasting about your skills or dropping AI
jargon. It comes through focusing on both relevance and the
relationship.

In the context of client outreach, you can do this with the 1-2
Punch strategy—a 2-part email sequence that leads with curiosity,
delivering value, and makes it easy to say yes.

Email 1: The Personalized Introduction
This is your first email. Its sole purpose is to start a
conversation by showing you understand their world.

Email 2: The Follow-Up
A second email sent 3-5 days later that reinforces your value,
builds on your first message, and prompts a response. Think of it
as a respectful nudge—not pushy, but persistent enough to show
you care.

“I did more research about AI consulting in general, I found a

“I wish I had followed Rob's prescription for the program exactly

As for how unique his path has been once he faced his fears?

Well, I’ll let you see it for yourself:

1 - The Spark (3-figures: $150).
A friend asked for help. He invoiced a modest consult +
mini-training (~$150). Tiny? Yes. But it flipped the switch from
“I’m studying” to “I’m in business.”

2 - A Real Project (4-figures: $7,000).
He kept showing up live -- workshops for nonprofits and small
businesses. Referrals started landing. His first automation build
(a dashboard for a local health-insurance company) came in around
$7,000, plus follow-on work discussing a fractional CAIO
retainer.

3 - Training Becomes a Lane (5-figures: $15,000).
He leaned into what he enjoyed most: teaching. When our AI
Consultant Directory was being announced, a company reached out
for help with AI training. That turned into a $5,000 package: two
full days, eight sessions (Fundamentals, Prompting Deep Dive,
Copilot Power Hour, “Build Your Own Bot,” and more) -- they even
flew him to New York to deliver it in person.

That first bootcamp went so well the client booked three more
(New York, Atlanta, Salt Lake City), plus consulting hours, for
$15,000 in additional work -- and started planning an org-wide AI
restructuring with him in the conversation.

4 - Five Figures in Five Days
A separate lead asked for a niche web app. He wrote the SoW,
shipped v1 in five days, and invoiced $10,000. His note to the
community: “I probably spent 20–25 hours, but it felt like zero
because I was having so much fun.”

5 - Please welcome to the stage…Professor Thiago 🎓🎉

His alma mater -- IE Business School in Madrid -- hired him to
teach in a four-part executive series on AI Strategy &

Professor and he’ll be teaching MBA alumni. That’s right, the
business experts are coming to him to learn. And then, the same
week he was lining up international workshop appearances while
visiting family in Brazil.

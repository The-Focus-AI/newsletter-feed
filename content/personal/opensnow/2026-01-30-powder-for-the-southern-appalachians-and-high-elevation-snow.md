Powder for the Southern Appalachians and High-Elevation Snow in
BC
*****************************************************************

A cold storm will impact the Southeast this weekend, with ski
resorts in the Appalachians scoring low-density powder. The rest
of the East will see bitterly cold temperatures. In the West, a
warmer and drier pattern will take hold for most areas while
storms continue to impact the Northwest. These storms will bring
rain & high snow levels, with higher elevation resorts in BC
seeing the best snow.

Snow for High-Elevation BC; East Coast Storm Possible this
Weekend
*****************************************************************

A series of storms will impact the Northwest and Western Canada
during the second half of the week, but snow levels will be on
the rise as warmer air arrives. Higher-elevation ski resorts in
British Columbia will be the most favored in this pattern. In the
East, temps will remain very cold this week, and a coastal storm
is also possible this weekend, though confidence in the storm
track is low.

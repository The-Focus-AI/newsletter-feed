Anthropic lets Claude negotiate real deals between employees, closes $4,000 in trades
12,049 Likes
Anthropic just ran a real marketplace where
AI agents negotiated on behalf of humans
, no human involvement once it started. Think Craigslist, but Claude handles all the haggling for you.
Here is how it worked: Claude interviewed each person, learned what they wanted to buy or sell, then went off and negotiated completely on its own. The results were solid:
186 deals closed
across 500+ listed items, totaling over $4,000
46% of participants said they would pay for a service like this
One agent, based on a passing mention of skiing, bought someone the exact snowboard they already owned
The big finding:
model quality matters more than your instructions
. Telling Claude to "negotiate hard" barely moved the needle. But Opus agents consistently beat Haiku agents on price, and the people with weaker agents never noticed.
That last part is the real story. If AI agents start handling real transactions,
you could be losing money without knowing it
, simply because your agent is cheaper.

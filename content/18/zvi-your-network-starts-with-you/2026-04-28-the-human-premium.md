# the human premium

**From:** "Zvi | Your Network Starts with You" <yournetworkstartswithyou@substack.com>
**Date:** Tue, 28 Apr 2026 14:03:20 +0000
**Source:** [View original](https://yournetworkstartswithyou.substack.com/p/the-human-premium)

---

View this post on the web at https://yournetworkstartswithyou.substack.com/p/the-human-premium

A colleague told me recently she’s stopped accepting LinkedIn connection requests from anyone she hasn’t met in person. Not slowed down. Stopped.
Her reasoning: she can no longer be certain a stranger online is actually a person.
That’s not paranoia. That’s a rational response to an environment where AI can generate a profile, write a personalized outreach message, and follow up thoughtfully, all without a human ever touching a keyboard.
The burden of proof has flipped. The default for a cold digital interaction is no longer “probably human.” It’s “not sure.”
what people are doing about it
I’ve written before about the growing need to actively prove you’re human in your outreach [ https://link.sbstck.com/redirect/857cd9c6-f815-4616-bbb4-15b1cd4f562d?j=eyJ1IjoiMWllZnkifQ.sG3OqnkHDNZpplC0eLA9u0NYhAE4J8yHwSPDyeVA8k8 ]. Specificity. Imperfection. Channel switching. Signals that a real person actually showed up.
But there’s a parallel shift happening on the receiving end. I set up my inbox so anyone saved in my Relatable [ https://link.sbstck.com/redirect/decfab1c-3509-431a-aa44-28059b360a1e?j=eyJ1IjoiMWllZnkifQ.sG3OqnkHDNZpplC0eLA9u0NYhAE4J8yHwSPDyeVA8k8 ] gets filtered to the top. Everyone else lands in the pile. I’m not ignoring people. I’m prioritizing the ones I’ve already verified as real humans I have a real relationship with.
My colleague’s LinkedIn policy is the same logic. So is the renewed appetite for warm introductions, trusted referrals, and in-person events. When you can’t trust the channel, you trust the person.
This is the human premium. The rising value of verified, known, trusted humans as the primary filter for your time, attention, and opportunity. It has always mattered. The premium is just going up fast.
what this means going forward
The people you most want to reach are building better filters. Getting through increasingly depends on whether you’re already in their trusted orbit, or whether someone in that orbit can vouch for you.
And you are also a filter. Who in your network do you trust enough to take a call for without a paragraph of context? That list is probably shorter than you think, and more valuable than you’re treating it.
As AI keeps flooding every channel we relied on, the relationships built on actual shared history and real trust become the ones that move things.
Here’s what I’ll leave you with: as you rely on AI more and more yourself, what will you put a human premium on? And the people you’re trying to build with, where do you think they’ll draw that same line?
Until next week,
-Zvi

# Makers of Modern China: An Online event on Zheng Xiaoqiong

**From:** The New York Review of Books <newsletters@nybooks.com>
**Date:** Tue, 28 Apr 2026 02:39:48 +1000
**Source:** [View original](https://thenewyorkreview.updatemyprofile.com/y-ahhdyud-36F2A313-iruduywlh-o)

---

One week from today, join Eleanor Goodman and Kaiser Kuo for a discussion of Zheng Xiaoqiong, a poet and writer who depicts the world of Chinese migrant workers. Zheng Xiaoqiong burst onto the Chinese literary scene in the late aughts, after years toiling in the factories of Guangdong. Last June, NYRB Classics published  In The Roar of the Machine [https://www.nyrb.com/products/in-the-roar-of-the-machine?_pos=1&_sid=cc85b17fe&_ss=r], Eleanor Goodman’s translations of a wide-ranging selection of poetry from across Zheng’s career. And last December, Equator, a new magazine of politics and culture, published  “The Makers of Modern China,” [https://www.equator.org/articles/the-makers-of-modern-china] a selection of chapters from Woman Worker, Zheng’s oral history of migrant workers in Guangdong, with a contextualizing introduction by Kaiser Kuo.This is a free virtual event co-hosted by  Equator [https://www.equator.org/] and  NYRB Poets [https://www.nyrb.com/collections/nyrb-poets].      REGISTER HERE [https://events.zoom.us/ev/ApCjwyuDcPvcMAbFEFD653UV7wwBFaGACAhqgjInZbyimh7QlRXT~Aj9PED8G31goq8hHbwbH7jsw_v9DqBFkBzHbD_BokQwhzhrLL19Ygn9f8Q]

Eleanor Goodman is the translator of seven books from Chinese and the author of two poetry collections. She is a recipient of a National Endowment for the Arts Fellowship and spent a year at Peking University on a Fulbright Fellowship. She is an affiliate of the Harvard Fairbank Center.

Kaiser Kuo is the host and co-founder of the Sinica Podcast, the leading English-language podcast on current affairs in China. He has lived between China and the U.S. for his adult life, working at major Chinese internet companies, as a journalist, and as a rock musician. He recently moved back to Beijing after nearly a decade in the States.
